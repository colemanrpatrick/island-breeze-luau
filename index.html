"He 'Ohana Kakou"
(We are ‘Ohana)
see island breeze luau come to life in the following video

“He ‘Ohana Kakou“is Island Breeze’s newest production. There is nothing more important in Hawai'i than 'ohana (family). The word 'ohana literally means "the offshoots" referring to the new shoots on our precious staple, the kalo (taro) plant - we are offshoots, forever connected to each other by our common root. Stories and cultural practices were passed down within the family so that today we can honor our 'ohana and share its beauty with others. We invite you to join us as we celebrate 'ohana. Meet our Polynesian cousins from Samoa, Tahiti & New Zealand. Hear our stories through song & dance as we present the splendor and beauty of this land we call paradise.

location

The Island Breeze Lū’au is held under the stars, on the historic grounds of the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel. The hotel is located adjacent to the pier in Kailua Bay, on Palani Road in Kailua-Kona. It is within walking distance of many restaurants, shops and historical sites such as Hulihe’e Palace and Moku’aikauwa Church in Kailua-Kona. The lū’au is easily accessible from the Kohala Coast Resort area to the north and the Sheraton Kona Resort & Spa at Keauhou Bay to the south.

Check-in Desk & Parking

The Island Breeze Lū'au reservation desk is located in the lobby of the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel. Vouchers must be turned in for lū'au tickets at the lū'au desk. Guests must have tickets to be admitted to the lū'au. Ample parking is available at the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel. Validation is given at the lū'au desk and during the lū'au.
